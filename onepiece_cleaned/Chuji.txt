Chuji
is the
pet
rat of the geisha
Some
.
Appearance
[
]
Chuji is a normal-looking rat who possesses light-colored fur and large round ears.
Personality
[
]
Chuji is a friendly rat who is loyal to its owner
Some
, coming to her when she calls it.
After being saved by
Sanji
, it seems to have become attached to him as well, and chose to sit on top of his shoulder and his head.
