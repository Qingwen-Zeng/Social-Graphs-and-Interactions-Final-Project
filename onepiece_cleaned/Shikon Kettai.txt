Appearance

Kettai in the
Digitally Colored Manga

Kettai is average-sized man with curly green hair and some beard stubble. He wears sunglasses and a black wide-brimmed hat with a skull on top of it. He also wears a red and orange vertically striped collared shirt, a white cravat, dark purple pants, and gray shoes.
Personality
[
]
Kettai likes seeing people's faces in surprise or fear. However, actually hurting people goes against his policy, so he decided to focus on spreading germs.
