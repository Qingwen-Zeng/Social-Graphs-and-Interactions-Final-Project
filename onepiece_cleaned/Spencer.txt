Spencer
was a member of the
Roger Pirates
until the crew's dissolution.
His current whereabouts and status are unknown.
Appearance
[
]
Spencer's concept art.
Spencer is an average-sized man with light skin, and light colored hair tied in a ponytail, with an even fringe and two strands framing his face. During his travels with the
Roger Pirates
, he wore a dark shirt with a light color jacket with several styled patches on the left arm, light colored pants, and flat light colored shoes.
Personality
[
]
Not much is known about Spencer's personality, but he was shown joyfully partaking in his crew activities.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
As a member of the
Roger Pirates
, Spencer is likely a formidable fighter and pirate.
Weapons
[
]
In his concept art, he is depicted with a sword sheathed at his waist.
History
[
]
Past
[
]
Thirty-eight years ago, Spencer and the
Roger Pirates
sailed to
God Valley
in order to fight the
Rocks Pirates
, in what was later labeled as the
God Valley Incident
.
25 years ago, the crew welcomed
Kouzuki Oden
into their ranks with a celebration.
During the crew's final journey to
Laugh Tale
, Spencer witnessed the
Road Poneglyph
on
Fish-Man Island
, and enjoyed the company of his crew. After the crew found all four Road Poneglyphs, he was part of the team to locate and chart their course and laughed with the rest of his crew when reaching there.
When
Gol D. Roger
left the crew, Spencer tearfully bid him farewell.
His whereabouts after his captain disbanded the crew are unknown.
