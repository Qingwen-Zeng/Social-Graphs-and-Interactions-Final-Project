Charlotte Harumeg
is the 33rd daughter and 73rd child of the
Charlotte Family
and the ninth of the
Charlotte Decuplets
to be born.
She is also an
officer
of the
Big Mom Pirates
.
Appearance
[
]
Harumeg is around twice as tall as her average-sized decuplet sisters. She has long brown hair which covers her eyes and is styled in bunches with a pink bow on top. She wears a pink sleeveless dress with a green ribbon, white gloves, horizontally-striped pink and white leggings, and red-pink high heels.
Personality
[
]
Harumeg was excited by the gathering at
Cacao Island
and noted the situation seemed like "fun".
Abilities and Powers
[
]
As an officer of the
Big Mom Pirates
, Harumeg has authority over lower-ranking members of their crew.
Rokushiki
[
]
In the anime Harumeg could maneuver in air as the males attacked Sanji, and she attacked Pekoms.
Weapons
[
]
Like the other decuplets, Harumeg carries a scythe with two spikes at the end of the blade.
