History
[
]
Long Ring Long Land Arc
[
]
At some point in her life, she either joined, or was won in a
Davy Back Fight
by the Foxy Pirates. She acted as one of the many shipwrights on the crew.
After
Luffy
defeated Foxy, Gina was one of the three shipwrights, along with
Donovan
and
Sonieh
, introduced as potential crew members for the Straw Hat Pirates to choose. Sanji asked if Luffy could take her as the prize, but Luffy ended up taking the crew's flag instead.
After the Davy Back Fight she and the rest of the crew sailed off as Foxy vows revenge on Luffy.
Sometime during the timeskip Gina left Foxy's crew for unknown reasons.
