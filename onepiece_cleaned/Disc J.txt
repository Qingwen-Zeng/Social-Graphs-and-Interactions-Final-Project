Appearance
[
]
Disc J in the anime.
Disc J is a stocky man. He wears many dark colored barbed accessories over his entire body.
In the anime, he was shown to have dark blue hair, and wore a tight black shirt, with his accessories colored green with yellow details.
Personality

Not much is known about Disc J's personality yet, but he is loyal to his captain and worries about his well-being.
