Usagihebi
is an animal who lives on the
Island of Rare Animals
.
Like all the animals on the island, he is protected by
Gaimon
.
Appearance
[
]
Usagihebi is a mixture of a snake and a rabbit, with a white and pink snake body and rabbit ears.
Personality
[
]
Not much of its personality is known, but it appears to be friendly as it is able to form a close bond with Gaimon.
History
[
]
East Blue Saga
[
]
Syrup Village Arc
[
]
Usagihebi was the first animal
Luffy
and
Nami
encountered on the Island of Rare Animals.
He was seen again with a group of animals when Gaimon waved goodbye to the departing
Straw Hats
.
Buggy's Crew Adventure Chronicles
[
]
Usagihebi,
Cocox
, and
Lionbuta
were there to witness
Buggy
's crash-landing on the Island of Rare Animals and the subsequent battle between him and Gaimon.
They then partied and danced along with Buggy. He then witnesses Buggy's departure along with Gaimon and other animals.
