Saru
is the pet dog of two of the
World Nobles
, Saint
Rosward
and Saint
Shalria
.
Appearance
[
]
Saru, like its World Noble owners, wears a helmet to "protect" itself from the outside air that commoners breathe. It has the appearance and color of the toy dog breed
pug
. However, its legs and body are longer than normal and its tail does not curl.
Personality
[
]
Saru behaves like a normal dog, but since it lives with the World Nobles, it is allowed to do whatever it wants.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
It has not shown any abilities in particular, but as the pet of a World Noble it likely has their privileges.
History
[
]
Sabaody Archipelago Arc
[
]
On the
Sabaody Archipelago
, Saru was walking alongside its owners when
Devil Dias
tried to escape his enslavement. The
Bomb Collar
exploded and brutally injured Dias. Saru then urinated on the injured man, and Shalria sarcastically reprimanded Saru's vulgarity towards Dias.
