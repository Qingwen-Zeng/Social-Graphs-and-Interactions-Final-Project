Yui
was a member of the
Roger Pirates
until the crew's dissolution.
His current whereabouts and status are unknown.
Appearance
[
]
Yui is a short man with wavy, light-colored hair that sticks straight up. He wears a light-colored cloak with a collar that is high enough to cover the lower portion of his face.
Personality
[
]
Nothing is known about Yui's personality.
History
[
]
Past
[
]
Thirty-eight years ago Yui was seen along with the
Roger Pirates
arriving at
God Valley
preparing to engage the
Rocks Pirates
in what would later be labeled as the
God Valley Incident
.
26 years ago on an island, the Roger Pirates defeated a battalion of
Marines
when they spotted the
Whitebeard Pirates
approaching them.
After the two crews battled each other for four days, Yui participated in a gift exchange.
After the Roger Pirates disbanded, it is unknown what became of Yui.
