Fugetsu Kisaburo
is a
swordsmith
from the
Wano Country
who forged the
Yakuza Kasen
. He is a member of the
Fugetsu Family
.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
Kisaburo is a master swordsmith, having forged one of the
12 Supreme Grade Blades
.
History
[
]
At some point in the past, Kisaburo forged the
Yakuza Kasen
. The sword eventually made its way off Wano, ending up in the possession of
Issho
.
