Hototogisu
is a
Marine
Vice Admiral
and a subordinate of
Vice Admiral
Tsuru
.
Appearance
[
]
Hototogisu is a regular-sized, young woman with light hair. Like many Marines, she wears her high-collared sailing coat draped over her shoulders. She also wears a bucket hat with the Marine logo engraved on it and a loose tie with her spotted shirt.
In the past, she wore sunglasses.
Personality
[
]
Hototogisu cares about Marine cadets and was willing to assault
Vice Admiral
Garp for giving what she saw as bad advice to them. She also denounces the idea of some lives being greater than others, and believes all lives have equal worth and should be respected as such.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
As a Vice Admiral, she has authority over lower-ranking Marines.
Weapons
[
]
During her presence at
Swallow Island
, she had a katana, but did not use it. On
Dressrosa
, she had a rifle strapped to her back.
Haki
[
]
Hototogisu can use
Haki
, due to all Vice Admirals possessing it.

[
]
Past
[
]
Hototogisu with Tsuru 13 years ago.
Hototogisu was one of the Marines present for the ambush orchestrated by
Tsuru
at
Swallow Island
13 years ago against the
Donquixote Pirates
, although the operation itself never came to fruition. She presumably accompanied Tsuru to
Minion Island
later on to confront
Donquixote Doflamingo
.
Two years ago, Hototogisu participated as an instructor in a training program run by
Garp
. He posed a dilemma to the recruits about whether to save an old man or a baby from an island of savage beasts, arguing himself that they should prioritize the baby. Hototogisu then physically assaulted Garp with a wooden bat and yelled at him for suggesting they should abandon the old man.
