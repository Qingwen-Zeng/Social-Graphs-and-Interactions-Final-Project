Unforgivable Mask
is a
hidden
One Piece
character, much like
Pandaman
. He is, in fact, Pandaman's rival.
Appearance
[
]
Unforgivable Mask's appearance is that of a muscle-bound figure with a panda face as a mask. He wears black swim briefs and boots. Unlike
Pandaman
(and others with a similar style), who appears to have an authentic panda's head, Unforgivable Mask actually appears to be wearing a mask.
History
[
]
Unforgivable Mask was hired by
Tomato Gang
to track down
Pandaman
.
