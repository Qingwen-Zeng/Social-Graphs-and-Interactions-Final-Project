Nosgarl
is an infamous
New World
pirate who allied himself with
Whitebeard
.
Appearance
[
]
Concept art of Nosgarl.
Nosgarl is an aged man with light hair resembling horns, glasses, and a hooked nose. He wears an old-fashioned pirate dress, with a mantle and heels. As many characters, his legs are disproportionately short and slender compared to his main body.

as he was willing to risk his own life trying to save
Ace
