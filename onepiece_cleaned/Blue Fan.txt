Blue Fan
is a member of the
Kuja Pirates
.
Appearance
[
]
Blue Fan's scheme from "Share the World".
Blue Fan is a short, chubby Kuja Pirate who wears skimpy revealing clothes. She has a round face and orange-brown hair. In her appearance in the opening,
Share the World
, she is dark skinned and blonde.
Personality
[
]
Blue Fan usually has a happy expression. She is a member of the Kuja Pirates, to whom she seems to be loyal to.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
As a member of the Kuja Pirates, it can be implied that she is very strong as only the strongest warriors are allowed on the Kuja Pirates' ship. Like all members of the Kuja Tribe, Fan can utilize
Haki
. It is still unknown specifically what type, though.

After

Later,

his brother
from execution, Blue Fan and the Kuja bid farewell to Hancock and Luffy as they boarded Momonga's ship and promised to help take care of the county in her stead.
