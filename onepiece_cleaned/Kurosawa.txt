Kurosawa
is a
samurai
who allied with the
Kouzuki Family
.
After waiting 20 years for the return of the
Nine Red Scabbards
, he affiliated with the
Ninja-Pirate-Mink-Samurai Alliance
to
raid
Onigashima
.
Appearance
[
]
Kurosawa is a middle-aged man of average build with tan skin, a large nose, and long eyebrows that curl upward at the outer ends. He often wears an expression with a frowning smile and furrowed brow. He has long black hair tied back in a ponytail, a widow's peak, and a long, black beard. Kurosawa wears a dark green kimono with a sun pattern on the sleeves, a dark collar, and striped
hakama
.
During the raid on Onigashima, he wears a light-colored kimono with a circular pattern on it and dark-colored hakama.
Personality
[
]
Kurosawa is loyal to the Kouzuki Family and was faithful for their return, waiting 20 years for
Kouzuki Momonosuke
and the Scabbards to return.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
Weapons
[
]
Kurosawa carries a
katana
.
History
[
]
Past
[
]
In the past, Kurosawa was allied with the Kouzuki Family. After hearing
Kouzuki Toki
's
prophecies
about the downfall of
Kurozumi Orochi
and the opening of
Wano Country
's borders, Kurosawa and his allies waited for 20 years for the Nine Red Scabbards' return.
During the Timeskip
[
]
After 20 years, Kurosawa and his comrades witnessed Momonosuke's return, and they swore their loyalty to the Kouzuki Family and offered themselves as allies to stand against Orochi.
Wano Country Saga
[
]
Wano Country Arc
[
]
Kurosawa was among those arrested and thrown in
Rasetsu Town
for conspiring to defy Orochi.
Two days before the raid on
Onigashima
, Kurosawa and
Jibuemon
discussed how to gather together without drawing too much attention.
After invading Onigashima and donning a
disguise
, Kurosawa joined Kin'emon's Eastern forces. The group split up after finding a
brothel
, but they heard
someone
from inside the brothel and all jumped in the water to hide.
After Luffy declared an all-out war on the
Live Floor
, Kurosawa and his allies abandoned their disguises and stood ready to fight the enemy crew.
Anime and Manga Differences
[
]
In
Episode 959
, the Kouzuki Family's allies, including Kurosawa and Jibuemon, met in
Kibi
to discuss the raid, but the location of their meeting was not shown in the manga. Kurosawa's lines from the manga are also spoken by Jibuemon instead.
