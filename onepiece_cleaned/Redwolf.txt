is a
Gifter
of the
Beasts Pirates

Appearance
[
]
Redwolf is a fair-skinned man with blonde hair, a square jaw and a cleft mouth. He wears frilled shoulder pads with a furred cape, a leather vest, a pair of dark brown gloves, gray pants with a belt, and boots. Due to eating a Wolf
SMILE
, he has a red furred wolf protruding out of his belly.
As a Gifter, he wears a pair of black horns.
Personality
[
]
Redwolf appears to be quite ambitious, wanting to be elevated to the position of
Tobiroppo
, and was willing to throw himself into battle to achieve his goals.
This is more explicit in the anime, where he yells that he intends to claim the position himself.
