Appearance
[
]
Beer VI is a middle-aged man with small ears, prominent cheekbones, thick lips and thick jawline. He has a thin black mustache and a goatee that end in three points.
He wears a dark red robe with brown fur trim on the cuffs and bottom, a jaguar-printed sash, and a blue cloak with a brown border and light blue fur trim. He also wears a black
ushanka
with white fur that has curved horns instead of ear flaps.
