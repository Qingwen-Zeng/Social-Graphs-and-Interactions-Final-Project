captain

Endurance Curry Pirates

Appearance
[
]
Hippie is a man with a light hair and a full beard connected to his sideburns. He wears a top hat with a skull attached to the front and a scarf wrapped around the crown. He also wears striped pants, a belt, a sailor tie and a captain's coat with no shirt.
Personality
[
]
Hippie dislikes spicy curry and prefers sweet-tasting curry.
He is aiming to find the
One Piece
.
