Akudai Kanzaburo
is an arms dealer who lives in the
Flower Capital
of
Wano Country
.
Appearance
[
]
Kanzaburo is man with curly dark brown hair, a semi-long beard and sideburns. His hair is styled in a pompadour at the front, while part of it is tied in a topknot at the back. He wears a light red kimono, dark green
hakama
with yellow swirling patterns, and a black and purple patterned cape.
Personality
[
]
Kanzaburo seems to be a nefarious individual, as he laughed heavily while discussing the production of weapons and their use for war.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
Kanzaburo's skills an arms dealer are highly evaluated, earning him an invite to the shogun's banquet.
Weapons
[
]
He used a naginata to attack
Nami
and
Shinobu
through the wooden ceiling.
