Charlotte Fuyumeg
is the 34th daughter and 74th child of the
Charlotte Family
and the tenth of the
Charlotte Decuplets
to be born.
She is also an
officer
of the
Big Mom Pirates
.
Appearance
[
]
Fuyumeg is an average-sized young woman with long brown hair styled in a braid with a pink bow on top. She wears a pink sleeveless dress with a blue ribbon, white gloves, horizontally-striped pink and white leggings, and red-pink high heels.
Personality
[
]
Fuyumeg seems slightly more reserved compared to her decuplet sisters.
Like the other siblings, she greatly looks up to Katakuri and was in shock over his defeat.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
As an officer of the
Big Mom Pirates
, Fuyumeg has authority over lower-ranking members of their crew.
Rokushiki
[
]
In the anime Fuyumeg could maneuver in air as the males attacked Sanji, and she attacked Pekoms.
Weapons
[
]
Like the other decuplets, Fuyumeg carries a scythe with two spikes at the end of the blade.
.
