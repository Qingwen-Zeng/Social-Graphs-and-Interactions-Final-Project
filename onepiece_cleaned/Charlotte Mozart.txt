Charlotte Mozart
is the 12th daughter and 31st child of the
Charlotte Family
and the elder twin sister of
Marnier
.
She is also an
officer
of the
Big Mom Pirates
.
Appearance
[
]
Mozart is a thin woman with very long dark maroon hair, which is partitioned into two large horn-shaped braids to the left and right of her head which resemble a jester's hat; parts of these braids are pink. She has purple eyes. She wears a pink collared shirt with horizontal purple stripes, a maroon skirt, striped tights, and a light purple cape.
Personality
[
]
Mozart is loyal to her family and crew, as she fought against the
Fire Tank Pirates
,
Straw Hat Pirates
, and
Germa 66
after they attempted to take out Big Mom.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
As an officer of the
Big Mom Pirates
, Mozart has authority over lower-ranking members of her crew.
Mozart possesses a strong will, being able to withstand a burst of Big Mom's
Supreme King Haki
and remain conscious from her loud scream.
Weapons
[
]
During the wedding, Mozart wielded a
Walker
.
