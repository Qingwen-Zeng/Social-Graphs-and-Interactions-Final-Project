Jibuemon
is a former retainer from
Kibi
serving under the
Kouzuki Shogunate
. As a
hatamoto
, he directly served under the former
shogun
,
Kouzuki Sukiyaki
.
Appearance
[
]
Jibuemon in his Beasts Pirates disguise.
Jibuemon is an old man, with several wrinkles on his face, a beard, and a two-part mustache. He has long hair with a ribbon tied over his head, and he wears a kimono with the sleeves collected and circular prints at the bottom.
Personality
[
]
Jibuemon highly treasures loyalty, as he continued to remain faithful to the Kouzuki Family, having waited 20 years for their return and readily offered his aid in opposing
Kurozumi Orochi
.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
As a samurai, Jibuemon had combat abilities that would have enabled him to protect the shogun. However, it is unknown if he is still capable of fighting after all these years.
History
[
]
Past
[
]
In the past, he was a retainer for
Kouzuki Sukiyaki
.
After the invasion of
Kaidou
and the downfall of the
Kouzuki Family
, Jibuemon was one of the witnesses who saw the burning of
Oden Castle
and
Kouzuki Toki
's
prophecies
about the downfall of
Kurozumi Orochi
and the opening of
Wano Country
's borders. Believing Toki's words, he searched for the remains of the
Nine Red Scabbards
at the burned castle. Having found none, he waited patiently for 20 years.
During the Timeskip
[
]
After 20 years, he was one of the first to recognize
Momonosuke
and welcomed him and his retainers back to Wano and swore his loyalty to the Kouzuki Family and told them that they still had allies in Wano after 20 years, all of whom have moon tattoos on their ankles.
