Hikkomitsukumai
is a retainer of the
Kurozumi Family
and a prison guard who lives in the
Flower Capital
of
Wano Country
.
Appearance
[
]
Hikkomitsukumai is an averaged sized man. He has a somewhat flat face, with long, blond hair, tied up in a topknot. He wears a light blue kimono with a patterned cloak over his shoulders.
Personality
[
]
Hikkomitsukumai doesn't seem to take the
Shogun
very seriously, scoffing at the idea that he would ever admit to making a mistake.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
As a prison guard, Hikkomitsukumai must have some fighting ability to keep the prisoners in check.
Weapons
[
]
Hikkomitsukumai wielded a spear, which he used to assist in guarding the inmates at
Rasetsu Town
.
History
[
]
Wano Country Saga
[
]
Wano Country Arc
[
]
Following
Orochi
's order to arrest 1,000 individuals suspected of being rebels, Hikkomitsukumai stood guard outside of the cell housing them. When it became apparent that the inmates were arrested by mistake (an intentional plot by the rebels), Hikkomitsukumai's co-worker,
Dashiteyaro
, questioned why they were still holding them, and Hikkomitsukumai scoffed and reminded him that Orochi would never admit to a mistake.
