Scimitarta
is a member of the
Endurance Curry Pirates
.
Appearance
[
]
Scimitarta is a man with curly dark hair covered by a turban with his crew's
Jolly Roger
drawn on the front.  He wears a striped vest and baggy pants, along with a jewel necklace and bracelets ob his right wrist and upper arm.
Personality
[
]
Scimitarta is excited at the prospect of finding the
One Piece
.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
Scimitarta possesses a flying
pirate flag
.
Weapons
[
]
Scimitarta wields a large ornate scimitar as his weapon.
