Appearance
[
]
Oran had black hair in a combover, a thin curled mustache, and slight stubble on his chin. He wore glasses along with a collared shirt, sweater, and bowtie.
Personality
[
]
Oran defended his niece from his wife's abuse and was the only person to show care for Robin, dealing with Roji's temper.
