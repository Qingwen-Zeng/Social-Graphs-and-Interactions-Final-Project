Island Eater
was a giant goldfish that inhabited the sea near
Little Garden
. He was said to be the main reason ships leaving the island never reached their destination.
He was classified as a Type C creature, "Big Savage".

Island Eater was an enormous fish, rivaling the size of even the most massive of
Sea Kings
. He resembled a typical white-red colored goldfish, with protruding eye sockets and a wide mouth that had a set of large teeth.
Personality
[

Little is known about Island Eater's personality, but he was considered highly savage in nature.
