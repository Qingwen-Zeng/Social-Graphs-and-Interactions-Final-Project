Rangram
was a member of the
Roger Pirates
until the crew's dissolution. His current whereabouts and status are unknown.
Appearance
[
]
Rangram's concept art.
Rangram is a tall, fit man with long, light-colored hair that he ties into a tight ponytail. He has sharp features and pointed ears. He wears a dark-colored vest, dark-colored pants, a light-colored wrap around his waist, a necklace, and light-colored shoes.
