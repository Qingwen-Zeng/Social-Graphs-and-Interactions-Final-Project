Beegirl
is a
Gifter
in the
Beasts Pirates
.
Appearance
[
]
Beegirl is a woman of average size. She has blonde hair (pink in the anime), and like other Gifters, has a pair of horns sticking out of it as well. She wears a yellow-black striped dress with the word "BEE" on the front. She has 6 arms and insect wings, likely as a result of her SMILE, and wears red gloves on each of her hands.
Personality
[
]
Beegirl seemed confident at the idea of defeating an enemy to become a
Tobiroppo
.
Later, after being tamed, she was happy to assist Luffy in fighting her own crew.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
Not much is known about Beegirl's abilities, but she seemed confident in her strength to defeat an unknown enemy.
Devil Fruit
[
]
As a Gifter, Beegirl has consumed a Wasp
SMILE
(
スズメバチの
SMILE
,
Suzumebachi no Sumairu
?
)
.
It appears to have given her two extra pairs of arms, as well as a set of insect wings.
Weapons
[
]
She was shown to wield a whip in each hand, which she used as her primary method of fighting.
