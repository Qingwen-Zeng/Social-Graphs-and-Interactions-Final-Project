Appearance
[
]
Usunoro is a muscular man with tanned skin and long blond hair, beard and mustache. Due to his
SMILE
ability, his lower body is a giant tortoise with light green skin and a dark green shell. He wears metal braces around his elbow, a black cape and a metal helmet.
Personality
[
]
Usunoro is quick to take the initiative as he attacked
Momonosuke
first out of all the Armored Division members, but does not pay enough attention to his surroundings, failing to notice
Yamato
before it was too late.
