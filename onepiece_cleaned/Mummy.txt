Mummy
is a sorcerer who joined as a gladiator at the
Corrida Colosseum
to compete for the
Mera Mera no Mi
.
Appearance
[
]
A full body view of Mummy in the anime.
Mummy is a wrinkled man, with bandages covering the sides of his face, making an X in the middle. His head is decorated with feathers. His hands are also wrapped in bandages, and he wears a fur covering, most likely made of an animal hide.
Personality
[
]
When
Cavendish
fell asleep, Mummy became very scared, since he knew that Hakuba would attack.
Mummy has a sense of honor, as he choose to lay his life on the line to protect
Luffy
from
Doflamingo
long enough to buy the
pirate
enough time to defeat the
Warlord

History
[
]
Dressrosa Saga
[
]
Dressrosa Arc
[
]
Mummy and the rest of D Block is defeated by Hakuba.
Mummy entered the
Corrida Colosseum
to compete for the
Mera Mera no Mi
. For the battle royale, he was placed in D Block.
As the battle royale began, Mummy was preparing to fight.
When twenty fighters remained,
Cavendish
's alter-ego Hakuba suddenly surfaced and proceeded to strike down all the remaining fighters in D Block. Mummy was hit by a blast of fire from
Damask
during the frenzy. Mummy then laid defeated on the arena floor.
After
Doflamingo
initiated his
Birdcage
and was nearly defeated by
Monkey D. Luffy
, Mummy and the other gladiators from the colosseum provided their aid to the Straw Hat after he became exhausted.
When the Birdcage came closer to the central streets, Mummy and the gladiators tried to attack Doflamingo, but they were easily defeated.
However, they did buy enough time for Luffy to recover, enabling him to defeat Doflamingo and bring down the Birdcage.
