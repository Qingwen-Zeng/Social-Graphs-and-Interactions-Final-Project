Moodie
is a woman who
Fullbody
took to the
Baratie
.
Appearance
[
]
Moodie is a relatively tall, slender woman, with blonde hair put back, with only two parts hanging down in front. She has brown eyes and wore light red lipstick. During the
Baratie Arc
, she wore a reddish-pink sleeveless dress that showed off her cleavage.
Personality
[
]
Moodie is a reasonable person who dislikes violence, as she tried to calm down
Fullbody
when he got angry and was horrified when
Sanji
beat him up.
History
[
]
East Blue Saga
[
]
Baratie Arc
[
]
Moodie accompanied
Fullbody
to the
Baratie
and dined with him. Enroute to the Baratie Moodie stops Fullbody from further attacking the
Straw Hat Pirates
. After they toasted, Fullbody tried to impress Moodie with his knowledge of
wine
.
Sanji
corrected Fullbody on facts about the bottle they were drinking, but Moodie still commented on Fullbody's knowledge. When Fullbody called Sanji over to the table to try to trick him, Sanji flirted with Moodie. Moodie laughed when Sanji made fun of Fullbody after the
Marine
pointed out a
fly
in his
soup
. She became distraught and tried to calm down Fullbody when he smashed their table in anger, and she watched in horror as Sanji beat him.
Moodie was nowhere to be seen when
Zeff
kicked Fullbody out of his restaurant.
