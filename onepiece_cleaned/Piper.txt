Piper
is a giant owl owned by
Gerd
.
Appearance
[
]
Piper is a massive owl, about the size of its master's upper body. Its feathers alternate between dark and light, with swirling ones around its eyes.
Personality
[
]
As Gerd's pet, Piper is very loyal to her. It also appears to have a high degree of intelligence, having witnessed something and then reported back to its master, even responding when asked about it.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
Due to its large size and intellect, Piper is capable of reconnaissance, and can inform its master of anything it witnesses.
