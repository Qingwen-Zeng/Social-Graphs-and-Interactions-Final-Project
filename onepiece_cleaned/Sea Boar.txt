The
Sea Boar
is a sea creature that debuted in
Hatchan's Sea-Floor Stroll
.
Appearance
[
]
The Sea Boar is a large ocean-faring creature, composed of a huge, bulky fish's body with a relatively thin dorsal fin and the head of a boar, complete with tusk, snout, and some hair on top.
Personality
[
]
The Sea Boar was terrified by the large sea monster that was about to attack it. It was so nervous that it regurgitated Camie and Pappag, suggesting that it has stomach troubles when it is scared.

[
]
Hatchan's Sea-Floor Stroll
[
]
After swallowing
Camie
and
Pappag
,
the Sea Boar was chased by a sea monster that wanted to eat it.
With the trident that he obtained from the
Goldfish Princess
,
Hatchan

the
mermaid
