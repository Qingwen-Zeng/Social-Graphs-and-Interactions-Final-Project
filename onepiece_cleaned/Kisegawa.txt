Kisegawa
is a geisha from the
Flower Capital
of
Wano Country
. She once possessed the blueprints of
Kaidou
's
fortress
on
Onigashima
.
Appearance
[
]
Kisegawa is a youthful-looking woman with large dark-brown eyes, Indian red lipstick and golden blonde hair that is gathered at the back with four, pastel flowers. She had light-red fingernails and wears an kimono with white and light-purple vertical stripes.
Personality
[
]
Kisegawa had a lack of understanding of what the
Onigashima
blueprints were about, leading her to use them as coasters.
History
[
]
Past
[
]
At some point in the past, Kisegawa received the blueprints for
Kaidou
's
lair
on
Onigashima
from an old man named
Kobe
. Not knowing what the blueprints were for, she used them as a coaster for a pot until a man named
Tokijiro
eventually acquired them from her.
Wano Country Arc
[
]
Franky
came to visit Kisegawa to look for the blueprints, and Kisegawa explained what had happened to them while in her possession.
Kisegawa celebrated during the
Fire Festival
alongside
Kumagoro
,
Kobe
,
Tokijiro
, and
Rakuda
, fantasizing with them about the
Kouzuki Family
defeating
Orochi
and
Kaidou
.
