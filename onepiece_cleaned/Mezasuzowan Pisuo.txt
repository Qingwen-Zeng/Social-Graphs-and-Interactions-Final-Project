Pisuo in the

Pisuo is a very large man, with a stocky build and a height many times that of an average human. He has thick lips, a large round nose, and short dark blue hair with dark purple spikes protruding from it. He wears a light green suit, a yellow collared shirt, and a red tie.
Personality
[

Not much is known about Pisuo's personality, as he has only been seen following his captain while keeping a blank expression.

[

Vito
