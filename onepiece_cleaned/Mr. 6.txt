Miss Mother's Day
and the top
Frontier Agent
in the
Baroque Works
rankings. He was first mentioned by
Nefertari Vivi
, who explained how
Baroque Works
was organized to the
Straw Hat Pirates
.
Appearance
[
]
Mr. 6's appearance is unknown, as neither him nor his partner are seen in the series. It is assumed that the number 6 plays a major part of his design, similar to the other
Frontier Agents
' designs.
Personality
[
]
Nothing is known about Mr. 6's personality, but it can be assumed that he is brutal and willing to kill to complete a mission like other agents.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
Nothing is known about Mr. 6's abilities, but his ranking suggests that he is considerably stronger than the other
Frontier Agents
.
