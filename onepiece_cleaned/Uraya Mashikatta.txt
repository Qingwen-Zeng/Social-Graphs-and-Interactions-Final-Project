Appearance

Uraya Mashikatta is a thin man with long, light brown hair, a large protruding nose, and a sharp jawline. He wears a blue hat and a crimson coat.
Personality
[
]
Little is known of Mashikatta's personality, but he was shown to be hostile towards the
Seven Warlords
system, cheering when it was abolished.
He appears to enjoy drinking, being seen sitting on the ground in an alleyway drunk; in the anime, he is surrounded by at least six bottles.
