is a
pirate
famed as a devious tactician, with a
bounty

17,000,000.
Appearance
[
]
Lacuba is a tall man with a brown coat and hat. He has long black hair and a beard, and has his crew's
jolly roger
tattooed on his chest.
After he attempted to commit suicide by biting his tongue off, the tip of his tongue was flattened.
Personality
[
]
Lacuba is a prideful man, as he would rather attempt suicide than spend the rest of his life being humiliated as a slave.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
Lacuba became famous for his tactics, so he is a skilled strategist. Being a captain, he has command over his crew.
