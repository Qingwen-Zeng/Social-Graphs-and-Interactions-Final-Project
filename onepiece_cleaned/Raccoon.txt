"Pretended Sleep" Raccoon
is a
West Blue
Pirate Captain
who was defeated by the
Whitebeard Pirates
.
Personality
[
]
Raccoon and his crew are greedy, as they tried to eat food in
Whitebeard
's territory without paying for it. Raccoon is also persistent, as he shot
Marshall D. Teach
after having been defeated and captured.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
Being a captain he is in command of his crew and his bounty of
75,000,000 is an indication of his dangerousness. But he and his crew were easily defeated by the
Whitebeard Pirates
and Raccoon himself was defeated by
Marshall D. Teach
very easily.
Weapons
[
]
Raccoon wields a
pistol
, and he is skilled enough to shoot Teach with it after being defeated.
History
[
]
One Piece novel A
[
]
Raccoon and his crew entered
Whitebeard's
territory and ate without paying for their meal before fleeing. They were defeated when the
Whitebeard Pirates
, led by
Ace

Thatch
, intercepted them and boarded their boat.
Teach
