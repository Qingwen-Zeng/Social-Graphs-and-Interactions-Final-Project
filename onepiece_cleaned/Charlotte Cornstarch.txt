Appearance
[
]
Cornstarch's original concept art.
Cornstarch is a large woman whose golden hair is slicked upward. She wears a fancy light blue dress with frills at the bottom, as well as a cream-colored cape with a very high collar that has vertical purple stripes.
Personality
[
]
Not much is known about Cornstarch's personality. However, since she took part in
Big Mom
's plan to assassinate the
Vinsmoke Family
, she can be considered very loyal to her family and crew.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
As the Minister of Love, Cornstarch has authority over
Loving Island
in
Totto Land
,
and as an officer of the
Big Mom Pirates
, she has authority over lower-ranking members of her crew.
Cornstarch possesses a strong will, being able to withstand a burst of
Big Mom
's
Supreme King Haki
and remain conscious from her loud scream.
