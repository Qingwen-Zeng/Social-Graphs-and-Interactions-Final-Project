Charlotte Anana
is the 39th daughter of the
Charlotte Family
, as well as the 85th and youngest overall child, and is a member of the
Big Mom Pirates
.
Appearance
[
]
Anana is a young girl with wavy pink hair that is split on either side and large red eyes.
She wears a short pink dress with a white collar and frills on the bottom, as well as black leggings and yellow shoes, and is always seen dragging a large plush bunny.
Personality
[
]
Despite her passive appearance, Anana seems to be sadistic and violent, as she wanted to use knives to mutilate her stuffed animal and has repeatedly popped the balloons her brothers
Dolce and Dragée
hang onto.
She also had no problem  dissecting living things as well as even humans.
