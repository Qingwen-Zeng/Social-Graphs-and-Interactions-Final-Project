Shimotsuki Family
under the
Daimyo
of
Hakumai

Shimotsuki Yasuie

Appearance
[
]
Megatenpanchiro is a light-skinned man with blonde hair combed into a widow's peak. It has small round eyes and red spiral patterns on its cheeks. He wears a light green kimono and a long sleeveless vest with high shoulder pads, with the Shimotsuki family symbol embroidered on the left side.
Personality
[
]
Megatenpanchiro took his job seriously and was concerned when
Shimotsuki Yasuie
chose to give money away to
Kouzuki Oden
without considering Hakumai's financial situation.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
Megatenpanchiro was in charge of accounting for
Hakumai
's funds.
