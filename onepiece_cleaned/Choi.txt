Choi
is an infamous
New World
pirate
who allied himself with the
Whitebeard Pirates
.
Appearance
[
]
A concept sketch of Choi.
Choi is an average sized man, with tanned skin and a black hat. His hat has a red feather on it, and he wears a black coat draped over his shoulders. His shirt is a light brown, with dark brown vertical stripes. He sports green pants, that show off his ankles and he wears brown normal shoes.
Personality
[
]
Not much is known of Choi's personality, other than that he was willing to fight to save Ace.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
Choi is a fearsome pirate who has managed to achieve an infamous reputation in the
New World
, and to make an alliance with the
Whitebeard Pirates
.
Weapons
[
]
He fights with a double lance but its ability is unknown.
History
[
]
Past
[

Some time prior to the current storyline, Choi became a known pirate in the New World, and allied himself with the Whitebeard Pirates.

When

Choi with Elmy, Pavlik and Rush destroying a Pacifista.

Elmy

Pavlik

Monkey D. Luffy

Emporio Ivankov

Hangan

Red Hair Pirates
