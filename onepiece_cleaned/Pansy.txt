Pansy
is a member of the
Kuja
tribe.
Appearance
[
]
Pansy is a large woman with round eyes, a double chin, and a big nose. She wears red lipstick, and has brownish-red hair, flowing in the back, and in two big curls in the front. She wears a wiggly black and yellow stripped top that shows most of her breast, and all of her stomach.
Personality
[
]
Not much is know about her personality, but like most other Kuja, Pansy has little knowledge and trust of men and of the outside world, and deeply admires their rulers, the
Gorgon Sisters
.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
As members of the Kuja tribe, it can be assumed that Pansy is a powerful fighter and knows how to use Haki. She was estimated to be stronger than
Poppy
, which she proved to be correct.
