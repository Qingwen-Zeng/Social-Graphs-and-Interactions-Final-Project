Acilia
is a gladiator at the
Corrida Colosseum
who participated in a tournament for the
Mera Mera no Mi
.
Appearance
[
]
Acilia's concept art from the anime.
Acilia is a large and bulky woman. She has dark colored lipstick, and dark colored hair. She is seen with a jolly smile, and sports an armored helmet.
Personality
[
]
Unlike most of the citizens of
Dressrosa
, Acilia does not scorn
Rebecca
for her heritage as
King Riku
's granddaughter. Acilia also advised her to have courage if she wished to win the Mera Mera no Mi.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
Physical Abilities
[
]
Acilia has an extremely high level of strength as in the anime, she could set Rebecca free from
Logan
's grip by striking his arm once, and with her shield, she could defend against his extremely powerful punches despite Logan's massive physical strength.
Weapons And Equipment
[
]
Acilia was seen wielding an axe and is highly skilled in using it as she defeated multiple powerful competitors in a few attacks.
She carries a shield she uses to fend off attacks from her enemies. She was able to block several attacks from Rolling Logan in the anime.
History
[

Dressrosa Saga
[
]
Dressrosa Arc
[
]
Acilia entered the
Corrida Colosseum
to compete for the
Mera Mera no Mi
. For the battle royale, she was placed in D Block.
Later, during the D Block round, Acilia and
Rebecca
partnered up and fought side by side. Acilia said that she wanted to make sure Rebecca reached the next round.
When twenty fighters remained,
Cavendish
's alter-ego, Hakuba, suddenly surfaced and proceeded to strike down all the remaining fighters in D Block. Acilia fell victim to his attack and laid defeated on the arena floor.
