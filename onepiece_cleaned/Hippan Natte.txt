Hippan Natte
is a
Waiter
of the
Beasts Pirates
who serves as a guard in the
Prisoner Mine
of the
Udon
region.
Appearance
[
]
Natte is a shorter man, with an average build. He has dark hair fashioned like the horns of a bull, and goggles that he wears on his forehead. He wears an open purple shirt that leaves his chest exposed along with a set of gray pauldrons on his shoulders, as well as a pair of blue pants.
Personality
[
]
Like his fellow Waiters, Natte is without honor, and has no qualms about attacking someone unable to defend himself if it means he can gain glory from it.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
Natte was fairly weak, as he was defeated without trouble by Kawamatsu.
Weapons
[
]
Natte wielded a
Sword
, and attempted to use it to kill Kawamatsu.
