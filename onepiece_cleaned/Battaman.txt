Appearance
[
]
Battaman is a man of larger size. He wears a blue helmet on his head, with earpieces, a thin mask over his face, and the letter "B" directly over the facehole("Σ" in the anime). Like other Gifters, he has a pair of horns sticking out of it as well. He wears a blue and white Beasts Pirates outfit with shoulderpads, and an arrow pointing down.
Personality
[
]
Battaman seemed confident at the idea of defeating an enemy to become a
Tobiroppo
.
Later, after being tamed, he was happy to assist the rebels in fighting his own crew.
Abilities and Powers

Not much is known about Battaman's abilities, but he seemed confident in his strength to defeat an unknown enemy.
