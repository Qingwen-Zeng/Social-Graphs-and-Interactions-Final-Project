Flower Capital

Appearance
[
]
Kumagoro is a man who appears to be of average height, and he has pale-purple hair gathered into a topknot. He wears a dark-orange kimono with lighter-orange vertical stripes.
Personality
[
]
Kumagoro seems to not be mindful of his money, as he let his rent pile up.
