Hiro Gomon
is a member of the
Endurance Curry Pirates
.
Appearance
[
]
Gomon is a man with a thin dark mustache and round eyeglasses. He wears dark baggy pants, an iron helmet and iron cape, bracelets, and shoes made from iron strips.
Personality
[
]
Gomon is a sadomasochist who loves torture, mainly inflicting it but also receiving it.
He is excited at the prospect of finding the
One Piece
.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
Gomon is heavily armored with an iron cape and helmet.
Weapons
[
