Appearance
[
]
As her name suggests, Lady Tree is a tree
homie
. She has full lips and long eyelashes, with her red foliage high on her head. She uses two slim branches as hands.
Unlike other tree homies from the Seducing Woods, she is seen wearing clothes, which in her case is a low cut, tight, yellow cocktail dress.
