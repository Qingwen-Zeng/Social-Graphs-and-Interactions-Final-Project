D.R.
was one of the main antagonists in
Oda
's one-shot,
Monsters
.
Appearance
[
]
D.R. has the letters of his name tattooed around his eyes in red and blue, and has short, tufty light purple hair. He wears a purple cape over his attire.
7 years before the events of
Monsters
, he had shorter spiky hair, his "DR" tattoos were smaller and on D.R.'s forehead, instead of around his eyes.
In his
wanted posters
in the anime, he is shown to have orange hair, and his tattoos are dark green.
In the manga, he was skinny. In the anime, he was fat.
Personality
[
]
D.R. is a cunning and dishonorable conman, plotting with his partner to frame an innocent
samurai
while they loot a town. When death is imminent, however, D.R. shows his cowardly side, using the fact that he is the only one who could prevent impending danger as leverage.
Abilities
[
]
It is mentioned that he is a third rate
swordsman
. He could also use the
Dragon Horn
to summon and control a
dragon
, a feat that took him three years to master.

Monsters

samurai
