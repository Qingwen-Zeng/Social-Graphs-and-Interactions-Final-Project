Appearance
[
]
Stefan is a white colored dog and bears a crescent-shaped white mustache similar to his master, Whitebeard.
Personality
[
]
Stefan, as Whitebeard’s dog, was loyal to him. He also enjoyed chasing and catching Whitebeard’s boomerang mustache.
History
[
]
Sometime in the past, Whitebeard took Stefan for a walk. During their walk, Stefan and Whitebeard played catch with the pirate's mustache.
