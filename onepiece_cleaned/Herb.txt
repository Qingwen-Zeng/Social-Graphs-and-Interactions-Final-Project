Herb
was a Shandia from 400 years ago. She was
Kalgara
's wife and
Mousse
's mother.
Appearance
[
]
She was an average sized woman, with black hair tied in a bun in the back, with decorative feathers in it and two strands hanging down. She wore a grey shirt, with white frills at the bottom, and a long reddish brown skirt that went to her ankles. Over her shirt she wore a small dark red coat, with no sleeves draped over her shoulders and buttoned with one button, and white fringe on the button.
Personality
[
]
She seemed to be a very good mother, as she did not want her daughter to be sacrificed to the gods.
History
[
]
Biography
[
]
At some point, she married Kalgara and they had a daughter, Mousse. Years later, she was dismayed that her daughter had to be sacrificed to appease the gods.
