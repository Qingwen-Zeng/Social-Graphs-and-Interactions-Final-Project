"Deep-Sea Currents" Umit
is a magnate who presides
Umit Shipping
and one of the emperors of the
Underworld
.
Appearance
[
]
Umit's concept art from the anime.
Umit is an older man with bags and age spots under his eyes. He has long black hair, a beard, and curled mustache, and he wears a large bicorne with anchor on the front and three feathers sticking out of the left side. He wears a brown coat over a ruffled white shirt, a yellow scarf, and wrinkled pants.
Personality
[
]
Umit appears to always be serious, in contrast to the joy and amusement his fellow emperors show.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
One of the cargo ships under Umit's command.
As an emperor of the Underworld, Umit possesses some amount of power within it. He is important enough to be invited to the
Emperor
Big Mom
's
tea parties
.
Being the president of
Umit Shipping
, he has full control over the company and its workers.
Vegapunk
made use of his shipping services during the construction of
Egghead
pointing out its proficiency in the maritime transport sector.
This implies that despite his ties with the Underworld, Umit enjoys enough prestige with the
Marines
to be entrusted with their shipments.
