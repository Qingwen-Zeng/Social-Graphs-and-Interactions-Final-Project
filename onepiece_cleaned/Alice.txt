Alice
is the current secretary of the
Galley-La Company
in
Water 7
.
Appearance
[
]
Alice is a 10 year-old girl with glasses and dark hair in a pigtail. For the secretary audition, she wore a dark-colored, double-breasted coat over a light-colored collared shirt, a pair of lace-trimmed socks, and bar shoes.
As
Iceburg
's secretary, she wears a pink pinstriped jacket over a red, long-sleeved, collared shirt and a short red skirt.
Two years ago at age 8, she was about a head shorter and had shorter hair.
Personality
[
]
Nothing much is known about her personality, but she seems to enjoy her job as she is frequently seen smiling.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
Alice's skills as a secretary are unknown, but they were good enough for her to receive the position among at least 156 other candidates.
