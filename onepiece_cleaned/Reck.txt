Reck
is a member of the
Kid Pirates
.
Appearance
[
]
Reck is a slim man with curly, parted hair and sporting eyeliner. He wears a leather jacket and knee-length pants.
Personality
[
]
Not much is known about Reck's personality yet. He is, however, loyal to his captain and attempted to protect him from
Big Mom
.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
Reck wields a pair of
flintlock pistols
.
History
[
]
Wano Country Saga
[
]
Wano Country Arc
[
]
Reck witnessed his captain fight Big Mom on the Live Floor. When it looked like Kid was done for, Reck was one of the crewmembers who attempted to defend his captain from Big Mom, but he only managed to get hit by
Hera
's lightning technique in the process.
Later, he, alongside other of his crewmembers, attempted to convince Kid to retreat when Big Mom was able to resist his
Damned Punk
technique at first, but Kid promptly ignored him.
