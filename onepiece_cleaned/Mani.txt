Mani
was a
combatant
of the
Bellamy Pirates
.
Appearance
[
]
She is a curly-haired, dark-skinned woman with thick lips and dark colored eyes. She sports a long sleeve white collar shirt decorated with blue feathers, dark purple pants and sandals.
Personality
[
]
She has a mocking personality, as she laughed at the
Straw Hat Pirates
for believing in a
Sky Island
.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
As a fighter of the crew, Mani may have some combat skills, but she, like the rest of the crew, simply stand on the sidelines while the two main fighters,
Bellamy
and
Sarquiss
, do most of the fighting.
