Bisley
is a
Shinuchi
of the
Beasts Pirates
and part of the
Armored Division
led by
Sasaki
.
During the
Raid on Onigashima
, he was tamed by the power of the
Kibi Kibi no Mi
and joined the side of the
Ninja-Pirate-Mink-Samurai Alliance
.
Appearance
[
]
Bisley is a rotund man with long, pale green hair and a short beard, both in the form of hedgehog spines. His nose is darker than his skin and he has a unibrow. He wears a dark green shirt and light green pants with purple suspenders. His belt buckle bears a skull insignia and the word "HARI" (針, "needle; spine").
Personality
[
]
Despite his playful expression, Bisley is quite timid and scared of enemies, only willing to fight from a distance.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
As a Shinuchi of the
Beasts Pirates
, Bisley has
authority
over lower-ranking subordinates.
Devil Fruit
[
]
Bisley ate a Hedgehog
SMILE
(
ハリネズミの
SMILE
,
Harinezumi no Sumairu
?
)
, an artificial
Zoan
-type
Devil Fruit
which transformed all of his hair into spines.
This offers him a form of defense against enemies, but does not protect him from ranged, shock wave-based attacks.
Weapons
[
]
Bisley uses a bow and arrows as his weapon, attacking enemies from a distance.

Heliceratops

Bisley was later seen along with other recovered members of the Armored Division asking Franky to help them escape the fire trapping them in the Right-Brain Tower.
Bisley and the rest of the Armored Division was eventually swept away by the torrent of water flowing throughout Onigashima while Franky yelled for them to hold strong.
