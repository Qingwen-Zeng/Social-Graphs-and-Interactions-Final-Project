Oide
is a little girl who lives in
Sphinx
. She is the owner of
Tama
.
Appearance
[
]
Oide is a little girl with blushy cheeks, brown pigtails and brown eyes. She wears a short, orange long-sleeved dress and a round wool hat with dots on the brim.
Personality
[
]
Oide is a cheerful girl who loves her pet, Tama.
Due to her young age, Oide does not seem to know about
Devil Fruits
, since she refers to the effects of
Marco
's
Tori Tori no Mi
as "magic".
