Biography

52 years ago, Pinzoro's village was attacked by
mountain bandits
. However, the villagers were saved by a group of migrants from
Wano Country
. Ten of the migrants chose to settle on the island and established
Shimotsuki Village
. Pinzoro married one of the settlers,
Shimotsuki Furiko

Roronoa Arashi
