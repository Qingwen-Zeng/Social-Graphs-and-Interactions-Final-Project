Ahho Zurako
is a
noble
from the
Goa Kingdom
and the daughter of
Ahho Desunen IX
.
Appearance
[
]
Zurako is a girl with a rather ugly face, reddish-brown hair with a yellow bow, and a light blue dress with white bows.
Personality
[
]
As a noble raised by a corrupted and spoiled parent, Zurako considers all people who are not nobles trash. She states that being burnt is their fault for not being born nobles.
Relationships
[
]
Ahho Desunen IX
[
]
Zurako had an amiable relationship with her father,
Desunen
. She full-heartedly agreed with him when he stated that the people who lived in the trash dumps of Gray Terminal deserve to die for not being born nobles.
