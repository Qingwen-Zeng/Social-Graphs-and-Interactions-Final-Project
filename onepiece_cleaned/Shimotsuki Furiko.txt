Shimotsuki Furiko
is a member of the
Shimotsuki Family
, a direct descendant of the legendary samurai Shimotsuki Ryuma and the older sister of
Shimotsuki Ushimaru
. She is also the wife of
Roronoa Pinzoro
, the mother of
Roronoa Arashi
, and the grandmother of
Roronoa Zoro
.
Appearance
[
]
Based on her silhouette, Furiko had long hair and prominent eyelashes.

Biography
[
]
Furiko was born in
Wano Country
, but illegally departed from there 55 years ago with her clansman
Shimotsuki Kouzaburou
,
Minatomo
, and 22 other people. Three years later, they settled down in what became
Shimotsuki Village
in the
East Blue
, where Furiko married a local swordsman named
Roronoa Pinzoro
. At some point, the couple had a son named
Roronoa Arashi
.
