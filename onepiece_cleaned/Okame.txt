Appearance
[
]
She is a skinny woman with red makeup on her cheeks and short black hair. She wears a long pink sleeveless dress, that has purple stripes around the waist, and the kanji ç ‚, meaning sand. She wears a white hat, and carries a large sword on her back.
Personality
[
]
Okame was already fond of Vivi as a youth before joining the band.
While she did not speak much she showcased bravery by attempting to protect Vivi from thugs.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
Okame is also seen holding a sword, though it is not known how skilled she is with it.
