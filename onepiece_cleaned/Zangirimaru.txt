Appearance

Zangirimaru is a man of average height with blond hair that forms large bangs around his face and is tied in a ponytail at the back. At Udon, he wore a standard prisoner outfit.
In the anime, he wears a light blue and blue striped kimono with a white

In the anime, he also showed himself to be a rather restless person, which forced
Hyougoro
