Appearance
[
]
The most prominent trait both of the brothers share are the large wind-up keys sticking upwards out of their heads, which they claim to have had since birth.
The younger brother
has a bulkier body and goes shirtless. He wears yellow gloves.
The older brother
is slimmer with visible abdominal muscle. He wears a purple shirt with the text "DOUBT" written on it (an open vest without writing in the anime).

[

The Doubt Brothers always act in sync.
