Hewitt
was the
chef
of the
Bellamy Pirates
.
Appearance
[
]
He wears a blue coat and has tan skin. His hair is black and has an angry expression. He also has the number "21" tattooed on the right side of his forehead.
Personality
[
]
He seems to be loyal to the Bellamy Pirates, and if so,
Doflamingo
as well.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
Hewitt is seen using a sword during him and his crew's fight with the
Saruyama Alliance
. As a
Cook
, he must have some form of culinary skill.

[
