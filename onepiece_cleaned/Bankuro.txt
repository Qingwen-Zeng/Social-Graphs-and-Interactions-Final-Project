Bankuro
was a member of the
Roger Pirates
until the crew's dissolution. His current whereabouts and status are unknown.
Appearance
[
]
Bankuro's concept art
Bankuro is a short, slender man with puffy, dark-colored hair and a beard. He has a pointed nose. He wears a dark-colored, short-sleeved shirt, a dark-colored hat with two horns sticking out of it, and baggy, light-colored pants.
In the anime, Bankuro's hair was white.
