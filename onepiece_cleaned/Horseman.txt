Horseman
is a
Gifter
in the
Beasts Pirates
and an attendant of
Bao Huang
.
Appearance
[
]
Horseman is an average-sized man with tanned skin and a visible abdominal muscle. Due to his
SMILE
ability, his head is that of a white horse with a gray mane. On his head, he wears a metal helmet with a yellow crest. He also wears a black leather vest, a fur coat, dark green pants, light brown gloves, and dark brown boots.
Personality
[
]
Horseman is a levelheaded and perceptive individual, keeping a close eye on his surroundings and warning
Bao Huang
when the
Shinuchi
is being careless.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
Horseman is a very fast runner, rivaling even
Gazelleman
, and is sometimes used to quickly deliver messages.

Midori Boshi: Devil
