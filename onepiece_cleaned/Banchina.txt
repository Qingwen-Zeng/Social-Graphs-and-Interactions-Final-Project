Banchina
was
Usopp
's mother and
Yasopp
's wife.
She died of a sickness when Usopp was still young.
Appearance
[
]
When she was sick, she was very pale. She had a long nose, which Usopp inherited, and black straight hair in the manga, while in the anime, her hair was dark green. She wore a pale, light green collared shirt.
Personality
[
]
Banchina was very kind and she really cared about her family. She was in love with her husband, Yasopp, and waited for him to come back home.
Banchina never lived to see Yasopp come home, or her son, Usopp, follow in his footsteps. She died when Usopp was very young, due to an unnamed disease.
History
[
]
Biography
[
]
Young Usopp trying to comfort his dying mother.
Banchina was left by her husband to follow his dreams and go to sea with the
Red Hair Pirates
. However, this caused Usopp to grow up with a sick mother who became bed-ridden due to her illness.
Every day Usopp would tell the same lie, that his father had returned to take them away, hoping it would keep her hopes high and help her to recover. However, she knew he would never return, and before her death, she told Usopp that she was glad he had gone out to sea.
Legacy
[
]
After Banchina passed away, Usopp continued to tell the same lie, even though he no longer had any reason to do so.
Anime and Manga Differences

Shanks
' men hoot and cheer like it was a good thing he left his wife and child.
