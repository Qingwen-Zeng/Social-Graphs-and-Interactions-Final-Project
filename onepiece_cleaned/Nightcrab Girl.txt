Nightcrab Girl is a tall slender woman with long dark purple hair reaching down to her waist. She wears a pink corset-like top, and a light pink skirt. Due to consuming a Crab
SMILE

As a
Gifter

In the anime, Nightcrab Girl appears to be wearing gray leggings, while in the
Digitally Colored Manga
, her legs are bare.

Nightcrab Girl appears to be fairly ambitious, being willing to attack
