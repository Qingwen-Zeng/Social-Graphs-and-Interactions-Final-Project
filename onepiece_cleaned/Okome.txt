Okome
is the
first mate
and the only known member of the
Hokahoka Pirates
.
Appearance
[
]
Okome is a tall, but fat man with wide cheeks compared to a thin forehead, but seemingly no hair. He wears a yellow tricorne with a bowl of rice from
Doskoi Panda
on top of it, a purple captain's coat over the rest of his unseen attire.
Personality
[
]
Little is known about Okome's personality, but he was the only person who witnessed
Bellamy
's
attack
on
Roshio
and managed to keep a straight face.

[
]
