Gattenshochinosukeo
is a
shipwright
from the
Wano Country
who assisted
Franky
in repairing the rebel's ships.
Appearance
[
]
Gattenshochinosukeo is a muscular man. He has brown hair that is tied in a topknot, and a short mustache. He does not wear a shirt, but does wear blue hakama pants. On his right shoulder is a floral tattoo.
Personality
[
]
Gattenshochinosukeo is a man with a keen intellect, easily being able to see the point of changing the rebel's riddle image.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
Gattenshochinosukeo is considered a first-class
shipwright
, with skills not far behind
Franky
and
Usopp
. He is also considered a skilled swordsman.

Kin'emon
