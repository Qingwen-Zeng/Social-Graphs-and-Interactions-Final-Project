The
Master of the Waters
is a large
Sea King
that inhabits the waters around
Long Ring Long Land
.
Appearance
[
]
The Master of the Waters is a giant yellow serpentine Sea King, with red spots and fins on its head and sides. It is huge, as all Sea Kings are. It has a lot of sharp, pointed teeth, two small eyes, a big tongue, and flat nostrils.
Personality
[
]
He is a renowned problem for passing ships and local folk around Long Ring Long Land. Like other Sea Kings such as
Lord of the Coast
, he is very aggressive and fierce. Most people who encounter him seem to be very afraid of him.
