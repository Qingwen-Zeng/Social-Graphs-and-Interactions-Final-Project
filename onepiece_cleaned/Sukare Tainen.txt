Sukare Tainen
is the
captain
of the
Germ Pirates
and the older brother of
Sukare Karehosui
.
Appearance
[
]
Tainen in the
Digitally Colored Manga
.
Tainen is a chubby man with black semi-long hair and a black chinstrap beard. He has scars running vertically from the edges of his mouth. He wears a light and dark purple striped shirt with a symbol resembling a flower with a face around his stomach. He also wears dark blue capri pants, black and white striped socks, brown shoes, brown gloves, a dark blue cape, and a dark purple bicorne hat with a golden rim and his
Jolly Roger
symbol.
Personality
[
]
Tainen appears to enjoy causing causing panic and chaos.
Relationships
[
]
Sukare Karehosui
[
]
Karehosui is Tainen's younger sister. She can be extremely selfish and tends to cause a lot of trouble for her older brother, but they still have a close relationship, as she was horror-struck when she saw him defeated.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
As the captain of a pirate crew, he has authority over his team and can give them orders. Independently, Tainen is relatively weak, as he was easily defeated by
Capone Bege
.
Weapons
[
]
Tainen possesses two
swords
and two
pistols
, the latter of which he uses to infect people with the
Kiss-Kiss Germ
.
