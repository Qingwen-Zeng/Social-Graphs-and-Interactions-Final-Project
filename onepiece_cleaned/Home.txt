Home
is a bull that acts as a steed for
Ashura Doji
.
Appearance
[
]
Home is an extremely large bluish-black bull with a bushy blue tail. It has a cloth with the Mt. Atama Thieves symbol tied around its neck with a red rope, as well as a piece of green fabric and a seat for Ashura Doji on its back.
Personality
[
]
Home is normally calm and well-behaved, but becomes distraught while running away from
Kaidou
.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
Befitting its size, Home is powerful enough to carry its equally large owner,
Ashura Doji
, with ease.
