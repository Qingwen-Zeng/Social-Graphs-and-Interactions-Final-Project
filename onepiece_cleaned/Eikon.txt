Eikon
is a dwarf from the
Tontatta Kingdom
and a member of the
Tontatta Pirates
.
Appearance
[
]
Eikon is small in stature and has a large fluffy tail, a pointy nose, and puffy cheeks. She has puffy dark hair that is held up with a bright hairband and wears a striped shirt with a fluffy collar.
In the
Digitally Colored Manga
, she has brown hair with a yellow hairband and wears a pink and purple striped shirt.
Personality
[
]
Eikon, like all the Tontatta dwarves, is very trusting towards others. Even though Nico Robin was perceived to be their enemy, she immediately trusted her when she claimed that she meant no harm and even gave her a warm welcome.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
As a dwarf, Eikon has strength and speed that is superior to that of an average human. However, she has not been seen directly participating in any combat.
Weapons
[
]
In the anime, Eikon wielded a spear.
History
[
]
Dressrosa Saga
[
]
Dressrosa Arc
[
]
After
Nico Robin
's capture by the dwarves and subsequent release upon them believing her to be a good person, Eikon smiled at Robin and stood behind
Gancho
after he introduced himself.
Later, Eikon and the other dwarves on
Green Bit
accompanied their new allies, Robin and
Usopp
, to the Riku Royal Army Headquarters, where they attended the meeting led by the
Thunder Soldier
announcing the start of their rebellion against
Donquixote Doflamingo
.
Anime and Manga Differences
[
]
In the anime equivalent of
Chapter 711
, Eikon's design is slightly different. Instead of a headband, she was shown wearing a white visor cap and holding a spear. Furthermore, she was shown attacking Robin after she revealed that she had no weapons to give them, instead of smiling at her after Gancho's self introduction.
