Muginn
is a giant raven that lives in the
Land of Gods
.
Appearance
[
]
Muginn is a massive raven, larger than a ship like the
Thousand Sunny
. It wears a proportionally large skull atop its head.
Personality
[
]
As a "guardian" of the Land of Gods, Muginn takes its job seriously, preventing anyone from leaving the diorama.
It is apparently very loyal to Road, as it delivers him people that it finds.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
Due to its massive size, Muginn has an advantage over smaller individuals. However, despite this, it was no match for
Roronoa Zoro
.
Muginn proved itself to have capable grip when it carried the
Thousand Sunny
a good distance.
History
[
]
Elbaph Arc
[
]
Muginn delivers the Straw Hats to Road.
Sometime during or following the
Straw Hat Pirates
' party, Muginn discovered their ship and flew it back to its master,
Road
.
When the Straw Hats attempted to escape from the
Land of Gods
, Muginn and its fellow "guardians" intercepted them. In the ensuing fight, Muginn battled against Zoro, although it stood no chance, to the horror of its master.
