Kikipatsu
is a member of the
Blackbeard Pirates
.
Appearance
[
]
Kikipatsu is a round middle-aged man with a  large nose and a thick, blond, fluffy beard that looks like cotton candy.
He wears a bandana, a vest and pants held by suspenders.
Personality
[
]
Kikipatsu appears to be a jolly and carefree man, showing no reservation while sitting right next to
Blackbeard
.
He loves drinking plum beer, but his nose turns red when he drinks it.
