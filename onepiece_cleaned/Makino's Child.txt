Makino's child
is an unnamed baby born to
Makino
and an unknown man during the two-year
timeskip
.
Appearance
[
]
The child is a small baby with short, dark green hair. It wears a yellow hooded bodysuit which covers its entire body apart from the hands and head. In the
Digitally Colored Manga
, its hair is dark brown and the bodysuit light blue.
Personality
[
]
As a baby, the child is not yet capable of speech and does not show much a personality outside of laughing and crying. It has recently learned to recognize
Monkey D. Luffy
's face from newspapers and seems to react positively to him.
