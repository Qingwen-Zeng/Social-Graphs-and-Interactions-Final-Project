Appearance
[
]
Saint-Marc is a very large man with tan skin, small head, and short hands. He wear a blue armor, with yellow epaulettes, red and white striped round elbow pads, and large blue gloves. He dons a purple cape with pink edges on his front, with a picture of a skull with an arrow pointing up. He also wear a blue Centurion helmet with a blue top that have two ivory straight horns on each side.
