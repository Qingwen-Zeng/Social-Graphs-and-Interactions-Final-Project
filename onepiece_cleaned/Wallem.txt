Wallem
is an infamous
New World
pirate
who allied himself with the
Whitebeard Pirates
.
Appearance
[
]
A sketch of Wallem.
Wallem is a man with a hat and a red pirate captain coat. He has long dark hair and a mustache with a goatee. He is seen wielding a sword.
Personality
[
]
No much is know about Wallem's personality but he put his own life on the line to rescue
Ace
from execution and was concerned when the
Marines
tried to execute him ahead of schedule.
He was overjoyed when
Luffy
saved Ace, cheering with his fellow allies.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
Wallem is a fearsome pirate who has managed to achieve an infamous reputation in the New World, and to make an alliance with the Whitebeard Pirates.

Summit War Saga
[
]
Marineford Arc
[
]
When Portgas D. Ace was to be executed at
Marineford
, Wallem and his crew joined forces with the
Whitebeard Pirates
to save him. Wallem and
Brew
noticed that the Marines were preparing to execute Ace ahead of schedule and became concerned.
Later, Wallem and his fellow allies cheered for Luffy, as he successfully saved Ace from being executed.
After
Akainu
killed Ace and
Jinbe
rescued Luffy, Wallem watched in surprise as
Crocodile
assisted them and ordered Whitebeard's men to catch them.
At the end of the war, the
Red Hair Pirates
arrived and Sengoku allowed the surviving pirates to leave.
Post-War Arc

Wallem, along with the rest of the Whitebeard Pirates' allies, attended Whitebeard's and Ace's funeral on an undisclosed island somewhere in the
New World
.
