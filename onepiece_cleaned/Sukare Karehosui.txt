Sukare Karehosui
is a member of the
Germ Pirates
and the younger sister of
Sukare Tainen
.
Appearance
[
]
Karehosui in the
Digitally Colored Manga
.
Karehosui is a petite young woman with long light blue hair. Her hair curls along her back, while longer bangs frame her face. The top of her hair forms antennae. She wears a short pink dress, a short white cape, and red high heels.
Personality
[
]
Karehosui is extremely selfish and causes a lot of trouble for her older brother. Nonetheless, she was horror-struck when she saw him defeated.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
Karehosui's individual strength is unknown, but she is unlikely to be very powerful as the
Germ Pirates
were quite easily defeated by the
Fire Tank Pirates
.

[

pistols
