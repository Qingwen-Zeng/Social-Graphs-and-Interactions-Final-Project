Hoisassa
is a retainer of the
Kurozumi Family
.
Appearance
[
]
Hoisassa is an average-sized man with a human upper-body and a horse lower-body. He has brown hair tied in a topknot, with a prominent widows peak. He wears a white kimono with the Kurozumi Family crest sewn into the sides.
Personality
[
]
Although he does not speak, it can be inferred from his demeanor that Hoisassa was obedient to
Kurozumi Orochi
, as he followed all of his orders without complaint.
