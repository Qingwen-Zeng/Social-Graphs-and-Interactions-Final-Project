Hanafuda
, also known as the
"King of Lizards"
, is a former
Warlord of the Sea
who was defeated by
Portgas D. Ace
four years ago. His Warlord position was given to
Bartholomew Kuma
after his removal.
Appearance
[
]
Hanafuda is a extremely tall and muscular man. He has thick light-colored hair, partly coming out from under a dark, pointed hat that covers most of his face. He wears a long, knee-length, dark jacket with several buttons and extremely prominent shoulder pads in the shape of raised elbows, which is unbuttoned and exposes his chest. He wears metal gloves on both hands, a belt that crosses his chest diagonally, light-colored trousers with irregular rectangular patterns with a wide metal belt over a fur-lined belt, and tall dark boots.
Personality
[
]
Not much is known about Hanafuda's personality, but he did have an interest in collecting
Ancient Zoan
Devil Fruits
.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
As a former member of the
Seven Warlords of the Sea
, Hanafuda was likely very powerful. However, he was defeated by
Portgas D. Ace
. He is said to have collected
Zoan
-type
Devil Fruits
, particularly Ancient Zoan-type, as a hobby, but it is unknown if he was a Devil Fruit user himself.
Weapons
[
]
Hanafuda wields a large
kusarigama
. Instead of the standard iron weight, his weapon is attached to a spiked metal ball.
History
[
]
Four years ago, Hanafuda was defeated by
Portgas D. Ace
, resulting in his removal from the
Seven Warlords of the Sea
and him being replaced by
Bartholomew Kuma
.
