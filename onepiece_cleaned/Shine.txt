Shine
is a
Marine Chief Petty Officer
who works under the command of
Hina
.
Appearance
[
]
Shine is a tall, tan-skinned man with broad shoulders who wears the standard Marine uniform of a white shirt with a blue ascot and a Marine cap. On his uniform, there are epaulettes that he wears on his shoulders to signify his rank. He has brown hair and a unique, two-pronged, black goatee that ends in a point. He also wears sunglasses and there is a tattoo of a ship on his left arm.
Personality
[
]
Shine is very serious about his work as a Marine, as shown when he enthusiastically gave a detailed report to
Hina
. However, he breaks his composure when confused or confronted by authority.
Relationships
[
]
Hina
[
]
Shine serves under
Hina
in her Black Cage Corps. He is very respectful towards her, as shown when he saluted her as he gave his report. However, he is also afraid of her at times, especially when she yells at him angrily.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
As a Chief Petty Officer, Shine has some authority over lower Marine ranks.
