Zou Arc

The Heart Pirates reunite with Law.
After Law arrived at Zou around a week later, the Heart Pirates happily reunited with their captain when he came into the Whale Forest.
That night, the Heart Pirates briefly met and greet
Monkey D. Luffy
before Law went to have a private conversation with the Straw Hats. The
Guardians

The next day, the Heart Pirates, some of the Straw Hats,
Kin'emon
,
Kanjuro

Raizo

Polar Tang
