Appearance

Kairiken's full appearance in the anime.
Kairiken is a large, tanned man with a prominent brow who wears a striped purple sweater and has a small afro perched at the top of his head.
Personality
[
]
As a bounty hunter, Kairiken is likely quite merciless. He was desperate enough to attack the Straw Hat Pirates but ended up losing miserably to them.

[

Not much is known about Kairiken's abilities, but as a bounty hunter he likely possesses some combat skill. However, he was easily defeated by
Luffy

Weapons
