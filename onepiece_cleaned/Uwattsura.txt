Uwattsura
is a
Waiter
of the
Beasts Pirates
who serves as a chief guard in the
Prisoner Mine
of the
Udon
region.
Appearance
[
]
Uwattsura is a muscular man with wild dark brown hair, a scar above his right eye and a tattoo with the kanji 鬼ヶ島 (
Onigashima
) on his right arm. He goes shirtless and wears goggles on his forehead, dark blue belted pants and brown boots.
Personality
[
]
He displays great confidence in himself and was eager to teach a lesson to the prisoners who had defied the guards.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
As a chief guard, Uwattsura possesses authority over lower-ranking Prisoner Mine staff. He was entrusted to fight against
Luffy
and
Hyougoro
in the
Sumo Inferno
, but was knocked out by Luffy's
Supreme King Haki
, displaying a lack of willpower.
Weapons
[
]
He used a sword while attacking Luffy and Hyougoro.
History
[
]
Wano Country Saga
[
]
Wano Country Arc
[
]
Uwattsura was among the Waiters who partook in the
Sumo Inferno
as Luffy's and Hyougoro's first opponents. However, right as the bout began, Uwattsura and the other Waiters were knocked out by Luffy's
Supreme King Haki
.
