Inukai
is a
Wano Country
official.
Appearance
[
]
Inukai is a man with very thick black eyebrows and a pointy black mustache. He has long brown hair that curls on the sides and is tied in a topknot at the back. He wears a light green kimono with small green dots, and a green coat with yellow-brown circle patterns.
Personality
[
]
Inukai admires
Kyoshiro
for his
yakuza
code and the fact that a courtesan like
Komurasaki
came from his pleasure hall.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
As an official, Inukai holds some political power and is influential enough to be invited to
Shogun Orochi
's banquet.
History
[
]
Wano Country Saga
[
]
Wano Country Arc
[
]
Inukai attended
Shogun Orochi
's banquet at Orochi Castle, where he conversed with
Kyoshiro
. Inukai first praised Kyoshiro for how well he had "tamed"
Komurasaki
, to which Kyoshiro replied that one should not refer to a prostitute like a dog. Inukai then brought up the incident of Kyoshiro summoning the
Tobiroppo
to deal with a
soba cook
, prompting Kyoshiro to explain his
yakuza
philosophy.
Anime and Manga Differences
