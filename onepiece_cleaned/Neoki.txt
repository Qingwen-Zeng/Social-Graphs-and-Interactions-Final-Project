Neoki
is a
Waiter
of the
Beasts Pirates
who serves as a guard in the
Prisoner Mine
of the
Udon
region.
Appearance
[
]
Neoki is a taller man, with a muscular build, and a pronounced jaw. He has puffy purple hair, and a metallic mask he wears over the top half of his face. He wears an open purple shirt that leaves his chest exposed along with a set of gray pauldrons on his shoulders, as well as a pair of blue pants. In addition, he has a tattoo on the lower-right side of his chest reading, "BOUTTO 4TEL".
Personality
[
]
Like his fellow Waiters, Neoki is without honor, and has no qualms about attacking someone unable to defend himself if it means he can gain glory from it.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
Neoki was fairly weak, as he was defeated without trouble by Kawamatsu.
Weapons
[

Neoki wielded a
Sword
, and attempted to use it to kill Kawamatsu.
