Blumarine
was a member of the
Roger Pirates
until the crew's dissolution. His current whereabouts and status are unknown.
Appearance
[
]
Blumarine's concept art.
Blumarine is a short, older man with a large chin, a dark-colored mustache, and sideburns. He wears a spotted bandana, a striped shirt, and light-colored pants.
Personality
[
]
Blumarine was concerned about
Kouzuki Toki
's health when she became ill.
