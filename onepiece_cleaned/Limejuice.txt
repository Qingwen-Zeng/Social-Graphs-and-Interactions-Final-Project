Limejuice
is one of the
Senior Officers
of the
Red Hair Pirates

Appearance
[
]
Limejuice is a relatively thin yet muscular man with blond hair. His trademark features are his nut-shaped hat with a Jolly Roger on it, and his white gloves, objects he retains in both of his appearances in the past and present. By the time of the
Summit War of Marineford
, he added to his attire a coat draped over his shoulders like a cape and a pair of sunglasses covering his eyes. His hair has grown longer, almost reaching down to his waist, and he has a stern expression.
Twelve years ago, in Foosha Village, his hair was shorter, and he was seen smiling.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
Limejuice is a Senior Officer of the Red Hair Pirates, giving him authority over lower-ranking members of the crew.
He is a powerful individual himself, considering how Marine Commodore
Brannew
described the whole crew as having a high average of bounties.
He is able to jump in mid-air similar to
Sky Walk
and
Geppo
.
Weapons
[
]
Limejuice holding an axe.
Limejuice primarily fights with an electrically charged staff, which he employs in aerial combat.
Limejuice was also shown with an axe in his past, although he has yet to be seen using it in the story proper.
