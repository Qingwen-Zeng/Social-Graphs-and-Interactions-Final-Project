Tate
is a
nurse
who worked for the
Whitebeard Pirates
as part of the crew's medical division.
Appearance
[
]
Tate is a young, blonde woman with bangs covering one of her eyes. She wears nurse attire, lipstick and leopard-printed stockings.
Personality
[
]
Tate cared for her captain,
Whitebeard
, showing concern for his excessive drinking. When Whitebeard went to Marineford ready to die in the
Summit War of Marineford
, Tate was left in tears.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
As a nurse, Tate has some degree of medical skill. She was capable of keeping the sick and aging Whitebeard in a relatively stable condition alongside a team of other nurses.
