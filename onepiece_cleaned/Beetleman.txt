is a
Gifter
of the
Beasts Pirates

Appearance
[
]
Beetleman is a tall man with blonde hair. Due to consuming a Rhinoceros Beetle
SMILE
, he has an extra pair of arms and rhino beetle horn protruding out of his forehead. He wears a blue cape that covers his mouth, two pairs of dark blue gloves, dark blue leather pants with a belt, and boots. As a Gifter, he also wears a pair of black horns.
His mouth, which he keeps hidden, is brush-like similar to a rhinoceros beetle.

[

He has a complex about his mouth and keeps it hidden under his cape.
