The
Needle God
is a giant hedgehog that lives in the
Land of Gods
, within
Bigstein Castle
.
Appearance
[
]
The Needle God is a massive hedgehog, with a friendly appearance. When enraged, it uses its spines to appear larger.
Personality
[
]
While normally looking friendly, the Needle God is easily startled, and will attack those who it perceives as threats.
This may be due to hunger, according to a caretaker.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
Due to its massive size, the Needle God has an advantage over smaller individuals. As a hedgehog, it has the ability to use its spines for offensive and defensive attacks.

[
]

[

Elbaph Arc
[
]
Sometime after
Nami
awoke within Bigstein Castle, the Needle God was wandering around and ran into her by chance. Due to Nami's reaction, it was startled, and pursued her. She eventually used
Zeus
to shock it, leaving it unconscious.
