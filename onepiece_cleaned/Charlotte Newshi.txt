Charlotte Newshi
is the 39th son and 68th child of the
Charlotte Family
and the fourth of the
Charlotte Decuplets
to be born.
He is also an
officer
of the
Big Mom Pirates
.
Appearance
[
]
Newshi is similar in appearance to his decuplet brothers, but noticeably taller and larger than the others. He has purple hair that goes down to his neck, small beady eyes, and a button nose. He wears a pink suit over a red shirt, a yellow tie, white pants, and a lilac bowler hat. In the manga, when gathered at Cacao Island, he wore a bow tie instead.
Personality
[
]
Newshi displays a more serious attitude compared to his playful decuplet siblings, and showed his reservations about gathering excessive amounts of strength to take on the
Straw Hat Pirates
.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
As an officer of the
Big Mom Pirates
, Newshi has authority over lower-ranking members of their crew.
