The
Basilisk
is the second-in-command of
Impel Down
's Level 2: Beast Hell, under the
Sphinx
.
Appearance
[
]
Basilisk's concept art from the anime.
The Basilisk is a gigantic chicken-like creature with a reptilian head. Its tongue sticks out in a deranged manner.
Personality
[
]
Being a giant snake born from a chicken, it shares traits of both animals.
Abilities and Powers
[
]
The Basilisk is well known and feared among the prisoners. All that is known is that it is weaker than the Sphinx.
