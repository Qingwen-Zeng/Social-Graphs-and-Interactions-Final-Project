Charlotte Normande
is the 38th daughter and the 82nd child of the
Charlotte Family
. She is a hybrid between a
human
and a
dwarf
.
Appearance
[
]
Normande is a very small girl with fair skin, purple eyes, and brown hair with yellow flowers in it. She wears a blue dress and has large, fairy-like wings. Like dwarves, she has a tail and her dress extends to cover most of it.
Personality
[
]
Not much is known about Normande's personality, but she enjoys playing with butterflies. She also appears to have a close relationship with her brother
Charlotte De-Chat
, as he allowed her to play on his hand.
